With three technical developers and one designer, Paul created an easy to use, beautiful site while gaining the respect of his co-founders an mentors. After selling in 2014, Paul was determined to become an expert developer.

PrepperPrepper

This app is created to help people make better emergency prepardness decisions based on where they live. People can select a city and get a synopsis of the risks from earthquakes, floods and civil unrest present in that city. Users can log in, easily input how much water, food and guns they have to get their prepper score based on what they should have for their citie's risks. Paul worked as a developer and PM in a team of four developers to build this site. It is an ongoing project.
